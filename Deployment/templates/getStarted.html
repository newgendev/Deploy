Hello and Welcome

Stroke or Cerebrovascular accidents is a medical condition that disrupts the blood supply to the brain, depriving it of oxygen and nutrients. It is one of the most deadliest diseases in the world. According to the World heath Organization upto 15million people globally gets affected.

Hence the objective of this webapp, W hich is to help medical professionals detect and classify stroke disease faster and more accurately, by passing CT images of the brain into our trained Deep Learning model.
